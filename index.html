    About Us

    Best Customer Experience

    Welcome to RideOn

    Your go-to destination for convenient and reliable car rentals. Founded with a passion for making travel accessible, we aim to provide top-quality vehicles and exceptional customer service to meet your every need.


    Our story:
    At RideOn, we believe that every journey is unique and should be stress-free. Established in 2018, we started with a small fleet and a big dream—to simplify car rentals for travelers, families, and businesses. Today, we're proud to serve hundreds of satisfied customers, offering a wide range of cars tailored to your lifestyle and budget.


    Our mission:
    To empower our customers with freedom and flexibility by providing safe, affordable, and well-maintained vehicles for any occasion.


    Why Choose Us:

    • A diverse fleet of cars for every need: economy, luxury, family-sized, and more.
    • Competitive prices with flexible rental plans.
    • Seamless online booking and 24/7 customer support.

    Join the RideOn family and make your next journey unforgettable. Wherever life takes you, we're here to help you get there!

    Why Choose RideOn?

    Diverse Fleet

    We offer a comprehensive range of vehicles to suit every need and budget. From economical hatchbacks perfect for city driving to luxurious SUVs for family trips, and everything in between. Our fleet includes economy cars, luxury vehicles, family-sized SUVs, hybrid options, and premium brands like BMW and Audi. Whatever your journey requires, we have the perfect car for you.

    Competitive Pricing

    We believe quality car rental should be affordable for everyone. Our pricing is transparent with no hidden fees, and we offer flexible rental plans including daily, weekly, and monthly options. We provide competitive rates starting from LKR 7,850 per day, with special discounts for longer rentals and seasonal promotions to help you save more on your journey.

    24/7 Support

    Our customer support team is available around the clock to assist you with any questions or concerns. From the moment you start browsing our website to the completion of your rental, we're here to help. Our seamless online booking system makes it easy to reserve your car, and our 24/7 emergency support ensures you're never alone on the road.
